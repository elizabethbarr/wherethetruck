Where the Truck

Make your food truck findable on Amazon's Alexa

List your location so your customers can ask, "Alexa, Where the Truck?"

Get your food truck on Alexa so your customers can find you, any day, any time

Listing your food truck or food truck event on Alexa doesn't require the investment of creating a custom skill. Where the Truck makes finding your daily location as easy as asking Alexa. Announcing your whereabouts on Facebook and Instagram is a crapshoot. You can't guarantee where it will show up on your customers' feeds. Listing your location with Where the Truck ensures you customers can find you without searching or scrolling. All they have to do is ask.
