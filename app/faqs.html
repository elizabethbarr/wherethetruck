FAQs

For food truck owners, food truck hosts, and organizers of food truck rallies, rodeos, and roundups:

Q: Do I have to create a custom Alexa skill to get my food truck on Alexa?
A: No. With Where the Truck, you can make your whereabouts available to Alexa users without the need for custom software development. Where the Truck is a subscription service that allows you to list as many food truck locations as you need.
Q: How do I get my food truck location info on Alexa?
A: Sign up here and start listing your locations right away. Your info will be available on Where the Truck's Alexa skill immediately.
Q: What does Where the Truck cost?
A: Where the Truck is $24/month for unlimited listings.
Q: Does Where the Truck work with Google Home?
A: Integration with Google Home (and other platforms) will be up next in version 2.0 of Where the Truck.
Q: Can I cancel my subscription?
A: Yes, you can cancel at any time.
Q: How many hungry food truck fans do I have access to with Where the Truck?
A: Millions! More than 47 million Americans have a voice-enabled device, and Amazon's Alexa has captured more than 70 percent of the smart speaker market. By 2020, 75 percent of U.S. homes will have at least one voice-controlled device.
Q: How do I edit a listing I already submitted? Or, there's a hurricane coming (or the truck broke down) and I need to cancel my listing.
A: Just give us a shout at at support@wherethetruck.is and we'll take care of it.

For food truck foodies:

Q: How do I use Where the Truck?
A: Just ask Alexa! Enable the Where the Truck skill in your Alexa app and then ask your Echo device, "Alexa, Where the Truck?"
Q: Is Where the Truck free to use?
A: Yes, Where the Truck is free.
Q: How does Where the Truck know where I am?
A: Where the Truck uses the ZIP code registered to your Amazon account. If you'd like to find food trucks elsewhere, tell Alexa another ZIP code.
Q: Does Where the Truck work with Google Home?
A: Integration with Google Home (and other platforms) will be up next in version 2.0 of Where the Truck.
Q: Why can't I find my favorite food truck?
A: Where the Truck tracks the location of participating food trucks. If your favorite food truck isn't listed, tell them to get on Alexa with wherethetruck.is.
